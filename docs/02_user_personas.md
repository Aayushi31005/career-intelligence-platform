# User Personas

This platform is designed for professionals across different stages of a technology career. While individual users may vary, their needs and challenges can be broadly represented through the following core personas.

These personas guide product decisions, feature prioritization, and user experience design.

---

## Persona 1: The Student / Final-Year Undergraduate

**Profile**
- Enrolled in a computer science or related degree
- Limited or no full-time industry experience
- Exploring roles such as Software Engineer, Data Analyst, or similar

**Goals**
- Understand which skills are actually required for entry-level roles
- Build a focused learning plan instead of following scattered advice
- Prepare a resume that aligns with real job expectations
- Gain clarity on how to approach networking without spamming professionals

**Challenges**
- Overwhelmed by conflicting online guidance
- Uncertain about skill prioritization
- Lacks confidence in resume readiness
- Limited professional network

**How the Platform Helps**
- Highlights high-demand skills for target roles
- Shows skill gaps and learning priorities
- Provides explainable resume alignment feedback
- Suggests low-friction networking paths (e.g., alumni connections)

---

## Persona 2: The Fresher / Early-Career Professional

**Profile**
- 0–2 years of professional experience
- Currently employed or recently employed
- Actively applying or preparing to switch roles

**Goals**
- Assess readiness for better or more specialized roles
- Identify missing skills that block career progression
- Improve resume alignment for specific job descriptions
- Network more strategically rather than randomly

**Challenges**
- Unsure whether rejection is due to skill gaps or resume misalignment
- Difficulty choosing between multiple learning paths
- Time constraints due to work and preparation balance
- Networking feels uncomfortable or ineffective

**How the Platform Helps**
- Evaluates resume-to-role alignment in an explainable way
- Differentiates between strong-fit and near-fit roles
- Provides prioritized skill improvement guidance
- Maps intentional networking paths relevant to target roles or companies

---

## Persona 3: The Experienced Professional

**Profile**
- 3+ years of industry experience
- Considering role transitions, specialization, or leadership paths
- Selective about job changes and learning investments

**Goals**
- Understand readiness for advanced or adjacent roles
- Identify skill gaps for role transitions (e.g., IC to specialist roles)
- Optimize resume positioning for senior-level opportunities
- Network purposefully with peers and role-adjacent professionals

**Challenges**
- Limited time for unfocused learning
- Difficulty gauging market expectations for advanced roles
- Resume feedback tools feel too generic
- Networking platforms prioritize visibility over relevance

**How the Platform Helps**
- Provides role-specific skill demand insights
- Highlights strategic learning opportunities with high impact
- Clarifies proximity to aspirational roles
- Suggests meaningful, context-aware networking paths

---

## Shared Characteristics Across Personas

Despite differences in experience level, all personas share common needs:
- Desire for clarity over guesswork
- Preference for explainable insights over opaque scoring
- Need for guidance without judgment
- Sensitivity to stress during career transitions

These shared characteristics strongly influence the platform’s design, tone, and interaction principles.
